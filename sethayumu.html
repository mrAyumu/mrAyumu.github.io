About

I'm a petroleum engineer with a focus on reservoir characterization, CO₂ EOR, and data-driven field optimization.

I specialize in transforming large-scale production data into actionable insights—using geostatistics, spatial analysis, and physics-informed modeling to improve decision-making in oil and gas field development.

My passion lies in applying modern data science to traditional petroleum engineering challenges. I recently presented my work at the SPE Canadian Energy Technology Conference, where I introduced a novel approach to correlating CO₂ EOR performance with geological features—laying the groundwork for a product I hope to continue building with future collaborators.

Whether it's fracture mapping, building interactive analysis tools, or guiding CO₂ EOR strategies, I bring clarity, precision, and a deep commitment to innovation.
